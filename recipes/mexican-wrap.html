Mexican Wrap

Description

Yummy mexican wrap with high protein and low kJ content.

Ingredients

  • High protein wrap
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mexican spice seasoning
  • Turkey breast mince
  • Corn
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Heat mince in medium to high pan.
  2. Add mexican spice seasoning and cook until brown/grey.
  3. Assemble all ingredients into wrap.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice over wrap for sauce.
  5. Enjoy.
